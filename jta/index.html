The Just Transition Alliance was founded in 1997 as a coalition of environmental justice and labor organizations. Together with frontline workers, and community members who live along the fence-line of polluting industries, we create healthy workplaces and communities.

We focus on contaminated sites that should be cleaned up, and on the transition to clean production and sustainable economies.

The Just Transition Alliance is a 501(c)3 organization based in San Diego, California.
